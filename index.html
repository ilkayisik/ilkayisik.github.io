Ilkay Isik

I'm a graduate student interested in understanding the mechanisms of aesthetic experience using functional brain imaging. I am currently working with Edward Vessel at Max Planck Institute for Empirical Aesthetics to understand how aesthetic experience can be explained as the result of activities at the network-level activities of the brain. I received my masters degree from Frankfurt University (Interdisciplinary Neuroscience) in 2015 and my BA from Bilkent University (Psychology) in 2013.
